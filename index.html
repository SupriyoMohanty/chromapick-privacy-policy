Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 July, 2024

No Data Collection

ChromaPick does not collect, use, or share any user data. All functionalities of ChromaPick operate entirely within your browser. Your color selections and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

Local Storage

All color history and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to any external servers and remains private to you.

Third-Party Services

ChromaPick does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
